About Kelleher Heartwood

Kelleher Heartwood is a custom woodworking shop based in Somerville, Massachusetts. Every piece — from furniture to signage — is designed and built one at a time, with an eye for the details that make handmade work worth the wait.

Whether it's a table built to fit your exact space, a sign that captures your style, or something entirely custom, I work with you from first sketch to final finish — picking wood, dimensions, and details together before anything gets cut.
